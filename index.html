Kill is a multi-media character, inspired by vintage ninja movies on VHS and Ninja Gaiden on the Nintendo Entertainment System (Cover art). He was created in 1994 when I, the artist Michael Nguyen (Visit the site) was in 6th grade (Photo).

Originally a comic book zine character of light-hearted gore and violence, Kill is now seen in original works, art prints, apparel, and his own video game called "Kill".

Shipping

  • Shipping and handling charges are estimated for the 3 options of United States, Canada, and International shipments.
  • We generally ship packages and mail via United States Postal Service (USPS).
  • All packages that are 13 ounces and under are shipped via First Class. Anything over that will be shipped via Priority Mail.
  • Priority Mail packages valued over $50 will be insured at its item value minus shipping & handling charges.
  • International orders may be tied up in customs and break estimated times of arrival.

Shipping Issues

  • Contact us via the Shop E-mail as soon as possible if your item(s) are damaged or lost. Provide photo proof if your item is damaged. The case will be investigated and we shall be in touch with followup steps.

Returns

  • Within 30 days, if you are not satisfied with your purchase and items are still in the condition in which they have arrived, you can e-mail us to arrange a return.
  • Qualified returns are subject to a 25% restocking fee.
  • We reserve the right to refuse service and/or limit quantities to anyone at our discretion.

Customer Privacy

  • All of who make purchases from the shop or make donations to Treehouse Studio are enrolled in the Historical Support Hall of Fame on the Credit page and you may also receive shoutouts on our Treehouse TV video series on Youtube.
  • No information otherwise is collected. We do not even have site analytic scripts running.
  • We are not in partnership with any third-party data harvesters.
