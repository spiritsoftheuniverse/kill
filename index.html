Kill is a multi-media character of the arts, inspired by vintage ninja movies on VHS and Ninja Gaiden on the Nintendo Entertainment System. He was created in 1994 when the artist Michael Nguyen (Site) was in 6th grade (Photo).

Originally a comic book zine character of light-hearted gore and violence, Kill is now seen in original works, art prints, apparel, and his own video game called "Kill". While still light-heartedly violent in nature, Kill is also represented in cultural mingling and studies of humanity. He is a catalyst for art which reflects those who are a part of Michael's life.
